Desi Music factory is an Indian music record label Incorporated on 31st December 2015. The two year old company was established by two friends Anshul Garg, a young dashing entrepreneur and Tony Kakkar , a talented Indian Singer, Music Director and Lyricist. The idea is to connect with all kinds of genre loving diverse audience. “There is no particular genre that works. People want good music and that’s all. Be it Jazz, party song, Bollywood song, it should sound good.” Says anshul.

So far the number says it all. Within a short threshold DMF outreached to 1.8 million subscribers on youtube and managed to become one of the fastest growing independent music label in India.

We have onboard with us some of the most prominent artists from India and Pakistan including Neha Kakkar, Tony Kakkar, Sonu Kakkar, Bilal Saeed , Young desi , Arjun Kanungo, Muhfaad, Sarmad Qadeer and many more.

Desi music factory Plans to launch nearly 50 song in the year 2018 and also will be looking forward to collaborate and work with more artists and reach all kinds of audience and genre lovers.

Anshul Garg

Born on May 8, 1992 Anshul garg is an entrepreneur from Delhi also the owner of a chain of roman bistro restaurant in Delhi.

Anshul”s journey with DMF started with his friendship with tony kakkar and neha kakkar who also performed for them in his restaurant “kimbuck2”.

He is the director and owner of DMF and all it's operations. He also featured in some of the home productions of the company like Coca Cola tu with karishma Sharma and Khafa mahiya sung by Tony Kakkar.

Tony Kakkar

Tony Kakkar is an Indian Singer, Music Director and Lyricist. The 1984 born made His Bollywood Singing Debut with Film Sawan Aya Hai (2004) and Punjabi Song Debut Akhiyaan (2015). Tony Kakkar Music Directorial Debut Mr. Bhatti On Chutti. He is Most popular for his unplugged song Mile ho Tum Ham Ko with his sister Neha Kakkar.

33 Years Old was, born in Rishikesh, Uttarakhand, India. His Father Rishikesh Kakkar and Mother Niti Kakkar. He has two sister Sonu Kakkar and Neha Kakkar both are singers.

His latest viral sensation “coca cola tu” feat. young desi made millions groove, grabbing 55 million views in just 1 month.
