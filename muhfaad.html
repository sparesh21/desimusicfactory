Muhfaad

Muhfaad a.k.a. Gaurav was born on 29th of May 94 in Delhi, India. Introduced himself as a performer at a very young age of 10. Muhfaad finally came out as a Hindi rapper in 2012 after getting focused for his life he left studies and dedicated his each and every second to music and decided to choose rap as his career. Muhfaad is the most versatile rapper in the scene so far known for his on stage energy, sense of humor and meaningful lyrics. Muhfaad has good writing command on Hindi, Punjabi, Haryanvi and English as well.
